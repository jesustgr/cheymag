ABOUT ME

Against the grain.

Even as a child, I always knew I was different. Not hanging with the girls. Getting down witht the guys. I never felt like I could truly be myself. I had these feelings of guilt, embarrassment for being different from everyone else around me growing up in Daly City. I found hope ina a place I would have never thought would be for me. The barber community. Ever since I entered this new world, I have gained brothers and relationships that I know will last me a lifetime. And for once, I truly felt like I belong. I finally began to embrace who I was as an individual and realized that me being different is my ultimate strength. I always had this big dream image in my mind of what it meant to make it out. I'll be living lavish, fancy car, cutting celebrities in somewhere like Los Angeles. Then I took a step back and really looked at everything I had right in front of me and realized I already am living the dream right here in my city, Daly City.
